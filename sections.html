About

This studio explores sculpture as a conversation between gravity, memory, and material. Clay, wood, metal, and found objects are used to build quiet forms that suggest pressure, repair, and transformation.

Work

Current work focuses on repeated vessel forms, surface residue, and the way objects can hold evidence of process. Each piece is treated as both an individual object and part of a larger field.

For studio visits, exhibition questions, or material conversations, reach out through the artist's preferred contact channel.
